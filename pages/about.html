The Firm

Sketch Concept Architects consists of qualified and experienced professional Architects, Interior Designer, Landscape Design and Planners. We carry out Architectural Consultancy services as applied to the building construction industry. Our firm is registered with the Board of Architects and Quantity Surveyors based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.

Design

Architectural Design Interior design & decoration Urban design. We have designed, developed high-impact projects for major clients across Tanzania.

Survey

Preparation of cadastral topographical surveys to enable establishment of area boundaries and preparation of contour maps in detailed views.

Project Management

Supervision of Construction work and issuing of payment certificates Invitations of contractors to pre qualify for tendering and preparation of tender reports.
